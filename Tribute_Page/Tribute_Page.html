Cacho Castaña

Humberto Vicente Castagna also known as Cacho Castaña

Argentine singer, pianist, songwriter and actor

Castagna perfoming at a live concert in Rosario in 2007










    Here is a timeline of Castagna's Life:

  • Born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in the Flores neighborhood
  • He devoleped an interest in music during his early years and became a piano teacher by age fourteen while working with his father as a shoe cobbler
  • He began his career in music as a pianist in Oscar Esposito's "orquesta tipica" a group who performed tango music a the time
  • His rise to fame began as a bolero singer in the 1970s, performing on Argentine television programs such as Tiempo Final and Un Cortado
  • He made numerous hits, including Me gusta, me gusta, Lo llaman el matador. He has composed around 2500 songs of which 500 have been recorded. Of the 44 records he has produced, fifteen became gold records and ten,platinum
  • He also starred in thirteen Argentine films like El mundo que inventamos and Los hijos de López
  • While he was a confirmed bachelor most of his life, he married 3 times, first with the actress and dancer Selva Mayo but the relationship was short-lived. Then he married Andrea Sblano, the 27-year-old daughter of a close friend. His last marriage was with Marina Rosenthal Cabrales, a pyscologist from Mar del Plata
  • He earned a Konex Award in 2005 for his work as a songwriter and his tango album released that year Espalda con espalda earned a Gardel Prize
  • In 2007 he was inducted into the National Academy of Tango
  • Althrough his career declined in the 90s,after 2003 he became active in Argentine's stage and festival circuit.
  • His health issues began when he developed emphysema due to his long time smoking habits and was hospitalized twice as he prepared to appear in shows
  • After two weeks hospitalized, he died on October 15,2019 from a pulmonary infection
